# air @ UF (COMING FALL 2026)

**air** is a University of Florida student organization focused on the frontier of physical AI. We're researching how machines perceive, predict, and interact with the physical world through visual learning and physical embodiment.

### Our Focus
We bridge the gap between computational intelligence and physical deployment:
* **World Models:** Training agents to predict physics and plan actions through simulated imagination.
* **Visual Robotic Learning:** Teaching robots complex tasks directly from raw video and visual streams.
* **Self-Supervised Learning:** Allowing autonomous agents to learn environmental dynamics without human-labeled data.
